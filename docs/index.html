SpeederReader

Read more books.

Our Mission

Too many books go unread. Not for lack of desire — most people want to read more — but because time is short and the habit is hard to build.

SpeederReader exists to change that.

We believe that reading is one of the highest-leverage things a person can do. Books are where humanity's deepest thinking lives — compressed into hours instead of lifetimes. Every book finished is a conversation with someone who spent years mastering a subject and chose to share it. Every book left unread is a missed conversation.

Our mission is simple: help people read more books. More books means more knowledge, more empathy, more imagination, and more literacy — not just in the sense of the ability to read, but in the deeper sense of being fluent in ideas.

We use RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) — a technique developed from decades of reading science — to help readers reach 2–3× their natural reading speed without sacrificing comprehension. A book that once took two weeks can be finished in a weekend. That changes the math. Suddenly, fifty books a year is achievable. A hundred is within reach.

Why Speed Reading?

The average adult reads at around 250 words per minute. The average non-fiction book is about 60,000 words. That's four hours of reading per book — assuming you sit down and focus, which most people don't.

At 500 WPM the same book takes two hours. At 750 WPM, just over an hour.

Speed reading is not skimming. Done correctly — with the right technique and practice — it is full reading at a higher gear. SpeederReader's RSVP method eliminates the eye movements that account for a significant portion of reading time, allowing your brain to process words at the rate it is actually capable of, not the rate your eyes allow.

Free, Forever

SpeederReader is free and open-source software, licensed under the MIT License. There is no subscription, no paywall, no premium tier. Your books never leave your device. No account is required.

We believe tools that increase human literacy and knowledge should be accessible to everyone, everywhere, regardless of income or circumstance.

Features
RSVP reading One word at a time, optimally positioned for instant recognition
Flow mode Slows automatically at punctuation to mirror natural reading rhythm
ORP highlighting Gold highlight locks your eye to the exact right letter in each word
Context strip Paragraph view keeps you oriented in the text
Word navigation Step forward and backward when paused
Dictionary lookup Look up any word instantly without leaving the app
Progress saving Your place is saved automatically
All major formats PDF, EPUB, HTML, Markdown, plain text
Fully offline No server, no cloud, no tracking
